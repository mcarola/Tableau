### Project 1: Global Health Insights Dashboard
**Dataset**: Gapminder Health Data  
**Live Dashboard**: [View on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/marvy.calderin/viz/GlobalHealthInsights_17652849913510/GlobalHealthInsights?publish=yes)

#### Business Context
Analyzed global health metrics to support a healthcare organization in identifying trends and disparities across countries and continents for strategic planning and resource allocation.

#### Visualizations Created

**1. Life Expectancy by Continent**
- Bar chart showing average life expectancy across continents
- Filtered to most recent year with sortable metrics
- Enables quick continental comparison

**2. Life Expectancy Trend Over Time**
- Line chart tracking top 5 countries with highest life expectancy
- Color-coded by country for easy differentiation
- Reveals temporal patterns and improvements

**3. Population Distribution by Gender**
- Interactive pie chart with country filter dropdown
- Percentage of total calculations
- Latest year data with single-value selection

**4. Life Expectancy vs BMI Analysis**
- Scatter plot exploring correlation between health metrics
- Color-coded by continent with interactive filters
- Individual country data points for granular analysis

**5. Custom Visualization**
- Additional insights using creative chart selection

#### Key Insights
- Identified health metric variations across continents and demographics
- Discovered life expectancy trends for top-performing countries
- Analyzed gender distribution patterns by region
- **Healthcare Application**: The NHS could leverage these findings to develop preventive medicine strategies focused on nutrition and population health habits

#### Technical Features
- Global filters across multiple worksheets
- Interactive parameter controls
- Quick table calculations for percentage analysis
- Published dashboard with public access

### Project 2: Spotify Music Trends Analysis
**Dataset**: Spotify Features (232,725 tracks)  
**Live Dashboard**: [View on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/marvy.calderin/viz/SpotifyTrends_17652946939780/SpotifyTrends)

#### Business Context
Analyzed Spotify's music library to uncover trends in popularity, musical characteristics, and genre preferences for music industry stakeholders and streaming platforms.

#### Metrics Analyzed
- **Popularity Index** (0-100): Track performance and play count
- **Valence**: Musical positivity score (happy vs. sad)
- **Danceability**: Track suitability for dancing
- **Energy**: Intensity and activity levels
- **Acousticness**: Confidence measure of acoustic content
- **Other Features**: Tempo, loudness, speechiness, instrumentalness, liveness

#### Key Findings
- **Top Genre**: Pop music dominates popularity rankings
- **Runner-ups**: Indie and Rap genres show strong performance
- **Interesting Discovery**: Anime genre shows concentrated artist following - the most popular artist is anime-related, demonstrating niche audience loyalty despite Pop's overall dominance
- **Trend**: Listeners of anime music show strong artist-specific preferences

#### Interactive Features
- Genre filters for comparative analysis
- Track-specific drill-down capabilities
- Multi-metric correlation views
- Dynamic popularity rankings

#### Business Applications
- Playlist curation based on mood (valence) and energy
- Genre trend identification for marketing strategies
- Artist recommendation engine insights
- User preference pattern recognition
